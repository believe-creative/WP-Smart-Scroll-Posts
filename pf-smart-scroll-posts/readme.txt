== Description ==
<strong>Smart Scroll Post </strong> is a <strong> FREE WordPress plugin,</strong> which allows you to load your infinite posts while scrolling. It is useful when you want your readers read another blog/post right after one just by scrolling. No need to go back and find next/previous posts/blog and click again. Just keep scrolling and keep reading. 
This plugin currently allows single post to scroll and load another


= Features: =
* Infinite load posts in scroll (Infinite scrolling, smart scrolling) 
* Easy to read articles/blogs without clicking article titles/next/previous etc. 
* Change URL of the post when it loads another (when you scroll) 
* Option to choose the default loader or upload your own. 
* Simple to use - just add a CSS CLASS to your code.
